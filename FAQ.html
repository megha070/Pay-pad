Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know!

  • What is PayPad Deposit?

    PayPad Deposit is the simple and safe alternative to traditional deposits. It is an agreement between three parties that keeps landlords' properties protected from damages and unpaid rent and helps renters avoid expensive upfront costs. Residents only pay if their Owners and property manager submits a charge.

  • What are PayPad deposit benefits over a traditional deposit?

    With a traditional deposit, the resident pays a large sum in advance, before there’s any actual reason for charging such an amount. With an PayPad, residents only provide secure and limited connection to their bank account and only pay if their property manager submits a charge.

  • How do I link my bank account?

    At the beginning of the agreement, Tenants can link their bank account through the E-Mandate equal to the security deposit required by the landlord or property manager. Money will only be automatically charged in the case of a claim.

  • How are the claims by the landlord approved and charged against me?

    As a policy, PayPad does not take sides in disputes and plays no role in assessing the correctness or fairness of each charge. Landlord provide basic information on damages and costs. PayPad always pays the Landlord or property manager for charges within 48 hours. PayPad’s intention is to provide sufficient time for residents to either discuss the charge with their property manager. Tenants have the legal right to dispute a charge just like they would have been able to do with a normal deposit.

  • What if the property is not partnered with PayPad?

    In this case, the customer can inform the landlord or manager about PayPad and they can set it up in their property by contacting us. They can contact us through our website and can register their property. The service is only available to partnered property.

  • Do tenants go through a screening process?

    To qualify for the PayPad deposit, Tenants will first have to meet the partnered property manager’s screening criteria. In addition, PayPad has partners who will help us carry a screening process on our behalf. We check the person background, financial and credit history.

  • Does PayPad require proof of damage before approving claims? How are disputes handled?

    As a policy, PayPad does not take sides in disputes and plays no role in assessing the correctness or fairness of each charge. Landlord provide basic information on damages and costs. PayPad always pays the Landlord or property manager for charges within 48 hours. PayPad’s intention is to provide sufficient time for residents to either discuss the charge with their property manager. Tenants have the legal right to dispute a charge just like they would have been able to do with a normal deposit.

  • Is PayPad a form on insurance?

    No, PayPad is not an insurance product but rather a credit product. If a property manager submits a charge, the property manager gets paid first, while the resident can repay in Instalments.

  • What if the resident simply can’t pay the claim?

    PayPad always pays the Landlord/property manager in full as soon as the charge is made, while the resident is able to pay in instalments. If customers do not pay back after constant reminders, then we report and reduce their CIBIL score. PayPad assumes the credit risk and suffers the loss if the residents defaults on these instalments.

  • How do I join?

    Tenants can join either by entering the partnered property on the website, can be referred by the Landlord or property manager.
